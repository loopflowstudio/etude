# Avatar Cube Team Sealed North Star

## North star

> "team sealed (1 human + 2 robots or 3 humans) vs 3 robots or 3 humans, on
> the avatar cube specifically as the format"

> "human constructs all 3 decks and picks one to drive"

> "we've gone too far down the product experience roadmap. we have a lot to
> build"

Etude Fantasia should grow toward Avatar Cube Team Sealed:

1. shuffle a versioned cube and deal one shared sealed pool to each team;
2. humans construct three non-overlapping decks and deck-specific sideboards;
3. a human chooses one deck to pilot while manabots may pilot the other two;
4. the teams play the full three-by-three deck matchup matrix;
5. the first team to five game wins wins the series;
6. every played game is recorded and studyable afterward.

The Avatar starting defaults are a 540-card cube, two 135-card team pools,
three 40-card-minimum decks per team, unlimited basic lands, three parallel
games where useful, and a five-win clinch. These are versioned format
parameters, not engine constants.

The first robot-only opponent may use fixed authored decks. A manabot that
constructs three legal decks from a shared pool is an important later
Intelligence capability. Drafting is separate and further away. Manabots do
not sideboard initially.

Discord is the assumed human communication layer. Etude does not need to build
chat. It must record each game under one team-series identity so all boards can
be replayed and studied later without merging their rules state or historical
information boundaries.

## Portfolio consequence

Study is part of the Game experience rather than an independent product
destination. Game owns the player loop from construction through play, replay,
and Study. Rules owns the authoritative Avatar world, pool and deck legality,
matches, identities, and exact continuations. Intelligence owns manabot play,
search, training, evaluation, and later deck construction.

This north star is not a request to file the whole product roadmap now. Current
work should continue to build the nearest runnable foundations: a polished
single game and replay, an expanding compiled world, runnable manabots and
search, and one honest Study loop. Team-series orchestration, arbitrary deals,
multihuman coordination, bot deck construction, and other downstream features
should become concrete only when the preceding system can run and measure
them.
