# Study (folded into Game)

## Objective

Study is no longer an independent product wave. It is the historical and
counterfactual time control of Etude Fantasia's AI-assisted testing house. A
completed game remains on the same table: every player decision can be
restored, and players can state a viewer-safe read, compare attributable
strategy, Retry an exact line, follow a bounded continuation, and return
without inventing rules or evidence in the client.

Live, watched, replayed, and Study views must preserve the same canonical
decision facts. The same belief, advisor, compute class, and provenance
identity produces the same advice at that decision. Facts, player-authored
beliefs, model-inferred beliefs, and advice remain separately labelled. Only
the acting pilot can commit a live offered `Command`; watching and isolated
Study exploration never pause or mutate the authoritative match.

## Process

Do not create new Study Projects or Tasks. Near-term player-facing work moves
to Game; exact branches and returns remain Rules work, and inferred beliefs and
policy/search evidence remain Intelligence work. Preserve completed Study
evidence as history. Defer the larger tree-navigation, room-sharing,
annotation, and poker-solver product roadmap until the first honest
play-to-replay-to-Retry loop works.

Discord remains the human communication layer. Etude owns the canonical
decisions, beliefs, strategy artifacts, and isolated actions that the testing
house discusses, not chat.
