# Study (folded into Game)

## Objective

Study is no longer an independent product wave. It is the post-game and
counterfactual mode of Game: completed Etude Fantasia games remain on the same
table, every historical decision can be restored, and players can inspect,
retry, compare, and return without inventing rules or evidence in the client.

## Process

Do not create new Study Projects or Tasks. Near-term player-facing work moves to
Game; exact branches remain Rules work and policy/search evidence remains
Intelligence work. Preserve completed Study evidence as history. Defer the
larger tree-navigation, sharing, annotation, and poker-solver product roadmap
until the first honest play-to-replay-to-retry loop is working.
