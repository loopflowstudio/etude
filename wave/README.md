# wave/

Etude's work is organized into **waves**: focused portfolios with a charter
(`GOAL.md`), running context (`MEMORY.md`), and numbered working documents.
A wave owns a surface and its contracts; tasks that change another wave's
contract live in that wave and appear here as dependencies. Charters bind to
Linear via their frontmatter.

| Wave | Owns |
|---|---|
| [rules/](rules/GOAL.md) | The curated-deck semantic kernel: typed card programs, exact identity, structured offers, proposed events, safe high-throughput forks |
| [game/](game/GOAL.md) | The authored playing experience: instant, legible, recoverable human-vs-manabot play over one versioned experience protocol |
| [study/](study/GOAL.md) | The engine-study experience: decision landmarks, retry before reveal, viewer-safe evidence |
| [intelligence/](intelligence/GOAL.md) | Policy, search, training, and evaluation: the measured research program toward superhuman play |

Retired portfolios are preserved under [archive/](archive/README.md) with
their dispositions.
